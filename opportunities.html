Field course in Kenya!

In collaboration with the organization Biomas, I'm organising a two week field course in Kenya called: "Socio-ecology of hunter-gatherer and nomadic populations in a changing world". The primary goal of this course is to expose students to the value of conducting fieldwork in socioecology, ethnoecology, or biological anthropology among subsistence societies, particularly those living nomadic lifestyles or that have only recently transitioned to sedentism.

Students will be introduced to key research questions and themes that can be explored in collaboration with several communities living in different geographical and ecological regions in Kenya, as well as the methodologies used to conduct such research in an ethical and rigorous manner.

We are planning the first edition to take place in summer 2025 - so stay tuned for news!
